Reco Fu 傅瑞乾

Systems Architect · Quant Engineer · Long-Horizon Operator

Career Narrative｜職涯敘事（不是標語）

EN
My career did not grow inside stable environments. It grew inside systems that were already breaking.

中
我的職涯不是在「正常運作」的環境中成長的， 而是在已經出問題、而且沒有人能接手的系統裡被迫成熟。

From early infrastructure work to quantitative systems, my role has consistently been the same: to stay when others leave, and make the system explain itself.

從基礎設施到量化系統，我的角色始終一致： 當系統失效、責任不清時，留下來把事情弄清楚。

Timeline & Responsibility｜時間軸與責任演化

Early Career — Owning What Others Avoided

I entered systems work not through greenfield design, but through legacy environments: undocumented behavior, fragile dependencies, and real-world consequences.

我不是從乾淨的新系統開始，而是從沒文件、會出事、不能重來的舊系統開始。

  • Cross-generation OS and network environments
  • Manual recovery before automation was possible
  • Learning cost paid in downtime, not theory

Mid Career — Automation Under No Supervision

The focus shifted from “keeping systems running” to “allowing systems to run without people.”

重點不再是「人盯著系統」，而是系統在沒人時仍然成立。

  • 8.92 years unattended automated trading execution
  • Failure handling without human intervention
  • Designing for boredom, not heroics

Critical Incidents — Where the Scars Came From

2015 — Cambodia Ransomware Incident
Factory-wide infection halted ERP operations.

Outcome: zero data loss, no ransom paid, full operational recovery.

2016 — Persistent Cluster Instability
Random crashes misdiagnosed at application level for months.

Root cause traced to Broadcom VMQ driver behavior under specific traffic patterns.

These were not “projects.” They were situations where failure had real financial and human cost.

What the Scars Changed｜傷疤改變了什麼

  • I no longer trust dashboards without behavioral evidence
  • I design systems assuming documentation will be wrong
  • I optimize for recoverability, not elegance
  • 我不再相信只存在於簡報中的系統
  • 我假設文件會錯、流程會斷、人會離開
  • 我優先設計的是出事後還能回來

Unfalsifiable Evidence Layer｜高成本證偽層

The following are not claims meant to impress, but statements that require real artifacts to refute.

  • Continuous execution across disasters and infrastructure failures
  • Merkle-hash verifiable records for trading history
  • Driver- and protocol-level root cause ownership

Why This Matters to an Employer｜對公司的實際價值

If your system fails quietly, I am not useful. If it fails publicly, legally, or financially — I am.

如果你的系統只需要「好看」，我不適合。 如果你需要的是有人能在事情變糟時留下來，那正是我的角色。
